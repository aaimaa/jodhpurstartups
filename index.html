Better Together

Our mission is to bring all the startups of Jodhpur under one roof so that we can evolve together, by helping each other through mentoring and by connecting with each other through events and conferences.

Idea

To make every startup comfortable during its journey to becoming a successful company.

Initiative

To give the startups a platform where they can improve their preformance.
